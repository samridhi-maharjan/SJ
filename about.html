About Bloom & Bliss

Our story, our passion, our people

Who We Are

At Bloom & Bliss, flowers aren’t just a product — they’re a way to connect with emotions, create memories, and spread love. Founded in 2020 in the heart of Kathmandu, we’ve grown from a tiny flower cart to a beloved neighborhood shop known for vibrant arrangements and heartfelt service.

Our Mission

We believe every bloom tells a story. Our mission is to make every occasion — big or small — a little brighter with the magic of flowers. Whether it’s love, gratitude, or celebration, we craft arrangements that speak your heart.
